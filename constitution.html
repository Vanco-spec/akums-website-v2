Our Constitution

The AKUMS Constitution defines our structure, principles, and governance. It reflects our commitment to integrity, representation, and accountability in all our affairs.

Guiding Principles

Every member is encouraged to read and understand the Constitution. It provides the formal framework that governs:

  • Membership rights and obligations
  • Election procedures and terms of office
  • Roles and responsibilities of executive officers and committees
  • Financial management, dues, and auditing
  • Meetings, quorum, and decision-making processes
  • Discipline, grievance, and amendment procedures

The Constitution ensures transparency, fairness, and continuity. Amendments are made through democratic processes and communicated to all members before enactment.
