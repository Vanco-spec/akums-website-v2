About the AKUMS Medical Magazine

The AKUMS Medical Magazine is the official publication of the Association of Kenyatta University Medical Students. It celebrates the intellectual, artistic, and social spirit of KU medical scholars through research articles, creative writing, interviews, and visual art.

Each issue highlights current trends in medicine, healthcare innovations, student experiences, and community impact stories. It’s more than a magazine — it’s our voice, our legacy, and our contribution to the medical world.

Our Mission

The AKUMS Medical Magazine is dedicated to publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed research and creative work that advances the understanding and practice of medicine. We strive to provide a platform for discoveries, innovative ideas, and reflective stories that shape the future of healthcare.

  • ✔ Rigorous peer review process
  • ✔ Indexed and accessible online
  • ✔ Open access for broader visibility
  • ✔ Student-led editorial and review board

Editorial Leadership & Ethics

Our editorial team upholds the highest ethical standards in publishing, following guidelines set by the International Committee of Medical Journal Editors (ICMJE) and the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).
